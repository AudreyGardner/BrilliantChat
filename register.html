Brilliant Chat is a fantastic way to meet new people. When you use Brilliant Chat, you are paired with another person at random to talk one-on-one. You will be paired with strangers who have compatible astrological signs (for example, Taurus --> Scorpio, and so on). You will be able to rate each other based on conversations at the end of each chat. Your ratings, as well as your astrological sign, will be displayed on the right. All new users will begin with a rating of 5 stars.
